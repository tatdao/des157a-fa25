Every Picture: Project Development

Thi Anh Thu Dao

November 4, 2025

At this stage, I decided to simplify the layout by removing captions for each photo and instead placing a flowing line of thought captions right beneath the images. I think it's more interesting if the slideshow of pictures stands alone itself.

  • A set of images slides automatically across the screen.
  • The captions slide in the opposite direction for a cool contrast.
  • The theme features simple interaction that an image is larger when moused over.
  • Minimal use of colors, including black, white and green-yellow colors.
  • The caption is seperated from images, telling what I see from the pictures and what these pictues tells about me.
