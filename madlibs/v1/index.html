A Rainy Day

It started raining at noon when I just woke up on the tree. The weather was crispy, and the sky looked orange. It made me nostalgic for the pancakes that my mom made when I live with her.

I ended up making myself a bowl of pens. It was delicious and disappearing in my mouth. I stayed home all day and worked on my assignments for tomorrow. It took me 24 hours to finish it.

The day seemed boring because I had nothing to do. I wish the weather would stop smoking so I could hang out with Bill.
